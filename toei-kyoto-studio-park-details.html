TOEI Kyoto Studio Park

4.8 (400)

TOEI Kyoto Studio Park is a unique theme park and film set located in Kyoto, Japan, designed to showcase the historical and cultural aspects of Japanese cinema, particularly period dramas (jidaigeki).​ Opened in 1975, it allows visitors to step back in time and experience the atmosphere of the Edo period through authentic sets, live performances, and various attractions. Guests can enjoy traditional ninja shows, dress in period costumes, and explore intricately designed streets that have been featured in numerous films and television series.

Details

Interest/Topic: Anime & Pop Culture

Popularity: High

Reservation: Not Required

Estimated Price Range: 2,200 - 3,200 yen ($20 - $30) for entry

Things to Do

Tips

  1. Highlights: Film set experience, traditional Japanese culture, live performances, interactive attractions.
  2. Duration: Year-round, with special events during holidays and festivals.
  • Explore Film Sets: Visit a recreated Edo-period town and watch jidaigeki films.
  • ​Live Performances: Enjoy ninja and samurai shows.​
  • Costume Rental: Dress as a samurai or geisha for photos.
  • Anime Museum: Explore Toei Animation exhibits.
  • Attractions: Try the Ninja Mystery House and other activities.
  • Plan Your Day: Arrive early to enjoy live shows and attractions.
  • ​Check Schedules: Review show times to avoid missing performances.​
  • Bring Cash: Carry yen for vendors and food stalls.
  • Dress Comfortably: Wear suitable attire and shoes for walking.
  • Stay Hydrated: Bring water and take breaks in calm areas.
